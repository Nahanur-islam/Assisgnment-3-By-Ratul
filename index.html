Hockey Training

Hockey training focuses on enhancing players' skills, agility, and endurance. It incorporates drills for stickhandling, shooting accuracy, and defensive strategies. Off-ice conditioning, including strength and cardio exercises, is crucial for overall performance. Training programs often emphasize teamwork, tactical understanding, and personalized skill development to prepare players for competitive play. Consistent and well-rounded training is key to fostering a player's growth and success in the sport.

Ski Touring

Ski touring provides an immersive winter experience, allowing enthusiasts to explore pristine landscapes away from traditional ski resorts. Participants ascend slopes using climbing skins, unlocking access to breathtaking backcountry terrain. Guided by experienced leaders, ski touring offers a blend of adventure, fitness, and appreciation for the natural surroundings. It's a dynamic and fulfilling way to embrace the beauty of winter while enjoying the thrill of descents in untouched snow.
